Drones for the middle of nowhere

The drone market is lacking options in the +6 hour flight time range. Existing products are excessively expensive, operate on fossil fuels and inaccessible to non-military organisations. The aim of AeroAlpina is to develop a fixed-wing UAV that can fill this gap.
